Hibachi Fried Rice

Home

Description

This hibachi-style fried rice recipe is quick and easy

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Grilled chicken
  • 1/2 Chopped green onion
  • 2 tbsp Chopped garlic
  • 2 cups Cooked white rice
  • 3 tbsp Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat wok over medium heat, add oil, cook and stir eggs for 2 to 3 minutes
  2. Add chicken, green onions, and garlic with scrambled eegs until heated
  3. Mix rice into eggs and stir until rice is warmed
  4. Add soy sauce, stir until rice is evenly coated for 2 to 3 minutes
