Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Basil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Stretch dough until flat
  3. Add Mozzarella Cheese, sprinkle Oregano, and add Tomato sauce
  4. Put in preheated oven for 4 to 6 minutes until crust browns
