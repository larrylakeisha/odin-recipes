Rigatoni

Home

Description

Rigatoni with a tomato cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Heavy Cream
  • Roasted Red Peppers
  • Parsley
  • Garlic Powder
  • Rigatoni Pasta
  • Green Peas

Steps

  1. Drain peppers, cut into strips
  2. Combine cream, tomatoes, peppers, parsley, garlic into pan
  3. Cover, cook over medium heat, stirring often until sauce comes into boil, reduce and simmer for 30 minutes
  4. Cook pasta according to package, drain, and transfer to large serving bowl
  5. Stir cheese and stir sauce into Rigatoni and top with peas
